Body and Excercise

human body contains 3 types of muscles.they are..

  • Smooth muscles (stomach, intestines etc).
  • Cardiac muscles (Heart muscles).
  • Skeleton muscles (Chest,triceps,biceps etc)

smooth and cardiac muscles are involuntary(which cannot be control over us).skeleton muscles are voluntary muscles(which can control by our mind). chest,triceps,biceps,abs,quads(thigh muscles) etc.. are the skeleton muscles. Walking, excercise, dancing, gym any physical activity are voluntary actions where skeleton muscles are invovled

Exercise/Workout

Exercise is any movement that makes your muscles work and requires your body to burn calories.There are many types of physical activity, including swimming, running, jogging, walking and dancing, to name a few.
